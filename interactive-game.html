The Birth of Jesus

The angel shone bright in the night sky, guiding the way with a radiant star that promised hope and salvation.

Shepherds and wise men alike looked up in awe, knowing that something holy had come to the world.

Mary and Joseph, faithful and humble, journeyed far to find a place for the newborn King.

Together they cared for baby Jesus, wrapped in swaddling clothes, the Son of God lying in a manger — the greatest gift of all.
