Learn more about other religions here.

Christianity

Faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior.

Jesus is the promised Messiah in the Bible, whose teachings and sacrifice form the cornerstone of salvation.

Muslimanity

Submission to God through the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad.

Islam emphasizes devotion to Allah, daily prayer, and the teachings in the Quran.

Hinduism

A complex blend of philosophy, ritual, and spiritual paths.

Key beliefs include karma, dharma, reincarnation, and various deities representing aspects of Brahman.

Buddhism

The path to enlightenment through meditation and moral living.

Founded by Siddhartha Gautama, Buddhism encourages mindfulness and detachment from worldly desires.
