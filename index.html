Learn More About Other Religions

Christianity

Centered on Jesus Christ. A message of love, forgiveness, and eternal life.

Christianity teaches that Jesus is the Son of God and Savior of the world. Followers believe in the death and resurrection of Jesus, and through Him, eternal life. Christianity values faith, grace, humility, and service. It is rooted in the Bible and the teachings of Jesus. (Source: EveryStudent.com)

Islam

Based on submission to God through the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad.

Islam teaches that there is one God (Allah), and Muhammad is His prophet. The Quran guides life, prayer, charity, and fasting. Muslims seek closeness to God through obedience and spiritual reflection. (Source: EveryStudent.com)

Hinduism

A diverse set of beliefs involving karma, dharma, and spiritual growth.

Hinduism is based on sacred texts and emphasizes self-realization, karma, and devotion. It includes worship of many deities but teaches all reality is one (Brahman). It promotes spiritual balance and harmony. (Source: EveryStudent.com)

Buddhism

Focused on enlightenment and peace through mindfulness and compassion.

Founded by Siddhartha Gautama, Buddhism teaches the path to enlightenment through the Four Noble Truths and Eightfold Path. It values non-violence, meditation, and compassion. (Source: EveryStudent.com)
