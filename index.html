Understanding the World Religions

Below are brief and respectful summaries of major world religions. We believe it's important to understand these differences so we can better see the light and love of Christ by comparison.

Christianity

Christianity is centered on the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Believers follow His teachings through the Bible and live in grace by faith.

Islam

Islam is based on the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad and submission to Allah. The Quran is the central scripture. It emphasizes discipline, prayer, and charity.

Hinduism

Hinduism encompasses various beliefs including karma, reincarnation, and devotion to many gods. It's known for its ancient texts and philosophical depth.

Buddhism

Buddhism teaches mindfulness, detachment, and the path to end suffering through the Eightfold Path. Its followers seek inner peace and enlightenment.
