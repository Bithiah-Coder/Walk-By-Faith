Our Church Story

Founded on the truth of Yahweh, centered in Yeshua (Jesus), and committed to love, growth, and truth. Walk By Faith began as a calling to teach the Gospel in spirit and in truth, following the King James Version of the Bible and the love of Christ for all people.

Understanding World Religions

Christianity

Christianity is centered on Jesus Christ, the Son of God, who came to save the world through love, sacrifice, and truth.

Islam

Islam is based on submission to God (Allah) and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad.

Hinduism

Hinduism embraces reincarnation, karma, and a deep sense of devotion and spiritual duty.

Buddhism

Buddhism follows the teachings of the Buddha and focuses on mindfulness, compassion, and peace.
