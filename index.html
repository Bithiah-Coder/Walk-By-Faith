Christianity

Centered on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. Stories emphasize love, sacrifice, and redemption.

Islam

Focuses on submission to God (Allah) and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad, promoting peace and charity.

Hinduism

Rooted in spiritual practices, karma, and devotion to various deities like Vishnu and Shiva.

Buddhism

Founded by Siddhartha Gautama, teaching mindfulness, compassion, and liberation from suffering.

How to Repent According to the KJV Bible

True repentance is turning away from sin and choosing to follow God's will. The Bible says in Acts 2:38, "Repent, and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the remission of sins..." Begin with heartfelt sorrow, confess to God, and surrender your life to Him. Jesus will forgive and cleanse you from all unrighteousness (1 John 1:9).
