Learn more about other religions here.

Jesus came to save all who would receive Him. Learning about others can help us understand our calling deeper while staying rooted in truth.

Christianity

Centered on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. Stories emphasize love, sacrifice, and redemption.

Islam

Focuses on submission to God (Allah) and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad, promoting peace and charity.

Hinduism

Rooted in spiritual practices, karma, and devotion to various deities like Vishnu and Shiva.

Buddhism

Founded by Siddhartha Gautama, teaching mindfulness, compassion, and liberation from suffering.

How to Repent According to the KJV Bible

Repentance is more than words. It's a turning away from sin, a heartfelt surrender, and an act of faith. Acts 3:19 says, "Repent ye therefore, and be converted, that your sins may be blotted out..." If you confess, believe, and turn your life toward Christ, He will forgive and restore you (1 John 1:9).
