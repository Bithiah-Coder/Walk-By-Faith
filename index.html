Our Church Story

Founded on the living Word of God, Walk By Faith Church is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through love, growth, and truth. We believe in the power of Scripture, worship, and fellowship as tools to transform lives and bring people into a deeper relationship with the Savior.

Understanding World Religions

This section shares brief, respectful overviews of world religions to help visitors understand the uniqueness of Jesus Christ through comparison.

Christianity

Christianity is built on the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Stories focus on God's love, His promises, and salvation through faith.

The Life of Jesus (KJV)

Birth: "Behold, a virgin shall be with child..." – Matthew 1:23

Baptism: "And Jesus, when he was baptized, went up straightway out of the water..." – Matthew 3:16

Teachings: "Blessed are the meek: for they shall inherit the earth." – Matthew 5:5

Miracles: "And Jesus went about all Galilee... healing all manner of sickness..." – Matthew 4:23

Crucifixion: "And they crucified him..." – Matthew 27:35

Resurrection: "He is not here: for he is risen..." – Matthew 28:6

Islam

Islam centers on the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad, emphasizing submission to Allah and acts of kindness and prayer.

Hinduism

Hinduism includes stories of deities and spiritual philosophies centered around karma, devotion, and the cycles of rebirth.

Buddhism

Buddhism follows the teachings of the Buddha, focusing on overcoming suffering, practicing mindfulness, and achieving enlightenment.
